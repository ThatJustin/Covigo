Frequently Asked Questions

You can change your password by following these steps:
  1. Open your user profile. You can access your profile by clicking on your username on the top right of your screen.
  2. In your profile, click on the "Edit" button in your personal information card.
  3. You will be redirected to the "Edit Account" page. Proceed to click on "Change Password" button.
  4. To change your password, enter your old password in the first field.
  5. Your new password must be 8 characters long, with at least 1 uppercase letter, 1 lowercase letter and 1 non-alphabetic character
  6. Enter your new password twice to make sure both entries match, and click on "Change Password" to confirm your changes
After following these steps you will be logged out of your account and will have to log in with your new password.
You can change your notification preferences and daily reminders settings by following these steps:
  1. Open your user profile. You can access your profile by clicking on your username on the top right of your screen.
  2. In your profile, click on the "Edit" button in your personal information card.
  3. You will be redirected to the "Edit Account" page. Proceed to click on "Edit Preferences" button and will be able to view your Account Preferences.
  4. To change your notification preferences:
    • You can change it in the "System Messages" checklist.
    • You can receive notification via Email or SMS. Select or unselect the message options according to your preference.
    • You can choose one, both or prefer to have none.
  5. To change your daily status update reminders setting:
    • You can change it in the "Status Update Deadline Reminder" list.
    • The list provides you durations from 1 to 6 hours. Choose the number of hours of advance warning to give for an upcoming status update deadline.
    • These reminders are sent only if you haven't completed your daily status update.
  6. After making your changes, click on confirm to save your changes.
After following these steps you will be re-directed to your user profile.
To contact your patient, you will have to:
  1. Open your user profile of your patient. You can access it through the dashboard.
  2. In your patient's profile, click on the "Message" button. You will be redirected to the "Compose Message" page.
  3. You will have to provide a brief subject to describing your reason for contacting your patient.
  4. Choose a priority (Low, Medium, High) depending on the urgency of your message.
  5. In the content section, describe the reason for message in detail, providing all necessary information for your patient.
  6. Once you are done click on "Create and Return" to send the message.
After sending the message, you will be re-directed to the "Messaging" Page
You can change your personal information by following these steps:
  1. Open your user profile. You can access your profile by clicking on your username on the top right of your screen.
  2. In your profile, click on the "Edit" button in your personal information card.
  3. You will be redirected to the "Edit Account" page. You can find your name, address and contact information on this page.
  4. You can make the changes that you intended to make
  5. Once your finished, click on "Edit and Return"
After sending the message, you will be re-directed to your profile, with a success alert on the screen, indicating the changes were saved.
You can add new symptoms by following these steps:
  1. Click on "Management", which is found on the sidebar.
  2. In the Management page, click on the "Manage Symptoms" button to open the symptoms pages.
  3. Once you are in the Symptoms page, you will find the "New Symptom" button on the top right.
  4. In the Symptom creation page, you can enter the name of the symptom and give it a brief description.
  5. Once you are done you can choose either "Create and Return" or "Create" buttons to create the symptom.
    • "Create and Return" button creates the symptom and opens the symptoms page.
    • "Create" button creates the symptom but keeps you in the symptom creation page to allow you to create more symptoms.
You will not be able to delete a symptom once created. However, you can do the following to the symptom you wish to delete:
  • You can edit the symptom to change it into another symptom.
  • You can disable the symptom. Disabling the symptom, makes the symptom unusable and can not be assigned to patients. They can be enabled if required later.
To assign symptoms to a patient, follow these steps:
  1. Open the profile of the patient you want to assign symptoms to.
  2. Click on the "Assign Symptoms" button, to open the Assign symptoms page.
  3. You will have to choose the date from when you expect the patient to start reporting their symptoms.
  4. You will also have to choose the duration for which the patient has to provide updates.
  5. You can then choose the symptoms that you expect to make updates on from the checklist of enabled symptoms.
  6. You will also have to choose whether the patient must quarantine.
  7. After you have assigned made the changes click on "Assign and Return" to assign the symptoms
The patient will be providing the Health Status Updates on a daily basis. In case the symptoms assigned need to be modified, the duration of updates needs to be extended or the quarantine status needs to be changed, you can follow the same steps. In step 2, it will be "Update Assigned Symptoms" button instead.
